How to Play

A test of your memory skill, Simon is a simple game to play. Each level creates a progressively longer sequence of colored buttons that you must remember and click in the correct order.


For example, Level 1 begins with a sequence of only one button. Click that button and advance to Level 2, where another button will flash. This time, you must click the button you clicked in Level 1 followed by the one that just flashed in Level 2.


Each level thus adds on to the initial sequence, and you must keep track of it. Simple, right? Now go see just how good your memory is!
